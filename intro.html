About Our Farm

Our farm is nestled in the picturesque Kedambady Village of Puttur Taluk, located in Dakshina Kannada Jilla. Agriculture which started with paddy cultivation has been cultivated in about 50 acres of horticultural crops like Areca Nut, Coconut, Banana, Black Pepper, Cocoa, Rubber, Scientific Cashew Farm.Spanning over 50 acres, it is a vibrant hub of diverse agricultural activities. The main crop is Areca nut, cultivated across 15 acres, accompanied by a thriving Rubber plantation of 15 acres, a Cashew plantation covering another 15 acres, and a Coconut plantation spread over 5 acres. We take pride in sustainable farming practices, supported by our cow farm, which produces bio-gas and a slurry system. This eco-friendly approach enhances soil fertility and promotes better crop growth, ensuring harmony with nature while sustaining productivity.

Achievements of Subhas Rai

Born in 1951 to Mundalaguttu Maila Rai and Padmavati Rai. Nephew of Freedom Fighter N.S Kille. B.A Graduate from Saint Philomena COllege Puttur in 1974.
Completed 10th standard education in 1968 and did agriculture along with college education.

  • Buntra Matrisangha Awarded 'Kapu Muddanna Shetty Memorial Gold Medal'
  • Dakshina Kannada District 'Karnataka Rajyotsava Award-2021'
  • 'Aranya Mitra Award' from Forest Department, Government of Karnataka,
  • Awarded the 'Best Farmer Award' at the national level workshop held in Goa by Cocoa and Cashew Development Directorate, a subsidiary body of Central Government, Kerala.
  • Puttur Taluk Level 'Karnataka Rajyotsava Award'.
  • D.K Zilla Kannada Sahitya Parishad awarder Taluk and District Level 'Literature Award'.

  • ODYOORU Sri Award - 2022.
